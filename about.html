About Us

About Us

Delta Health Care Chittagong Ltd.

Delta Healthcare, Chittagong Limited Chattogram first fully air-conditioned state-of-the-art hospital and diagnostic center. Which is located between Katalganj Mirzarpul and Panchlaish thana. Incorporating the latest and most advanced technologies and concepts in medical care in keeping with the times is part of the routine work of this hospital. The 120-bed hospital has facilities to examine about 3,000 patients per day and provide outdoor treatment to 1,200 patients to ensure proper treatment and services to patients. Computer Based Automation System. Well-equipped with own water treatment plant, VVIP, VIP CABIN, automatic fire extinguishing system and automatic lift.

Our Vision

Our vision is to reach a level of excellence in health care services through a sustained effort to quality service and to achieve a global standard through the indoctrination of a culture of excellence.

Our Values

We are devoted to ensure quality in all our endeavors and to maintain the highest ethical standards in our business practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to benefit people and improve their quality of life through our quality services. We would like to ensure patients satisfaction through providing quality health services at affordable cost, launching new ideas by exploring all possible avenues. We are committed to achieve our goal through skilled, creative and motivated employees of highest professional standard.

Our Object

Our object is to conduct transparent business operation within the legal & social framework in order to attain the declared vision.
